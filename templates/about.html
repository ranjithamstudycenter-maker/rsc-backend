About Ranjitham Study Center

Ranjitham Study Center is a dedicated mathematics tuition center providing structured coaching for Class 6 to Class 12 students. Our mission is to build strong fundamentals and help students approach mathematics with confidence.

We follow a concept-based teaching approach. Each topic is explained step by step, ensuring students understand the basics before moving to advanced problem-solving.

Regular homework, worksheets, and weekly tests help students improve accuracy, speed, and exam readiness.

Individual attention is given to every student. Slow learners receive special care, while high performers are encouraged to achieve excellence.

Our Teaching Style

  • Concept clarity with simple explanations
  • Exam-oriented preparation
  • Regular tests and revision
  • Personal attention and doubt clearing

Students from Class 5 to Class 12
